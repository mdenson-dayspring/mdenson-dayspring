Grace Fellowship Group Bike Ride
August 18, 2018

This is information about the group ride I'd like to take with friends.

Who

You are invited to come.

All ages. If you can ride a bike or scooter please come.

When

August 18, 2018

9:00AM - 10:00 AM

What

This is intended as a fun ride! Not a serious workout! If a workout is what you want you can ride to and from the start point.

We will be riding on the Bay Trail starting in Burlingame and going South through Coyote Point. The route is chosen to have minimum interaction with automobiles. Hopefully, none.

It will not be a fast or long ride. We will keep track of everyone and agree to turn around as a group when people are ready.

Where

We will start just North of Coyote Point on Airport Road. There is parking on the road and at the Anza Fisherman's Park.

What to Bring

This will be a short ride so you won't need much except a bike and ride to the start point.

Bringing water in a bottle would make sense.

I'd say bring a sun hat, but you are going to wear a helmet right?

I will bring minor first aid kit in case of scrapes.
